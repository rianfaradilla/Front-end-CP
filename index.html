About Us

We've been making delicious food last for 10 years

House of Dimsum is a halal dimsum restaurant that is served with the best taste at an affordable price. We present a variety of dimsum and food menus that are guaranteed to be delicious, healthy, and halal. Our mission is to be the most sustainable restaurant in Jakarta by sourcing our ingredients locally, supplementing produce with herbs grown on our garden.
